# Code of Conduct
We expect all contributors and participants to adhere to the following code of conduct. This code of conduct outlines our expectations for behaviour within the project community, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behaviour.

## Our Standards
We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and participants. We expect all members of our community to respect each other and conduct themselves in a professional and courteous manner.

Specifically, we do not tolerate:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind, including but not limited to age, race, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or national origin.
- Intimidating or threatening behaviour, or any behaviour that creates an unsafe environment for others.
- Disrespectful or derogatory comments, personal attacks, or any behaviour that causes others to feel unwelcome or uncomfortable.
- Any form of dishonesty or unethical behaviour.

## Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior
We take all reports of unacceptable behaviour seriously. Any member of the community who engages in unacceptable behaviour may be asked to stop or leave the community, at the discretion of the project maintainers. In more serious cases, we may report the behaviour to appropriate authorities.

## Reporting Unacceptable Behavior
If you witness or experience any behaviour that violates this code of conduct, please report it immediately by opening a confidential issue or contacting the project maintainers directly. All reports will be kept confidential and will be reviewed promptly.
